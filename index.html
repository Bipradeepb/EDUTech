About Us

Why the Education Industry?

Education is the one of the founding pillars of the society. Education is the transmission of knowledge, skills, and character traits. Edutech was established in Nov 21,2014 by a team of young,technically and morally driven team of entrepreneurs. Now we are a family of over 20,000+ education professionals. When Edutech was founded it was done so to bring an impact to the society. What better way to bring social revolution than to nurture and guide the young minds of tomorrow. Hence the education industry. We now target students and peer groups of every age group. We now bring education to the doosteps of everybody who is eager to learn. So if you are ready to learn with technology, Edutech is the answer.

Technology Driven. Socially motivated.

Edutech has since the days of its establishment has always been clear of its goals to educate people in the booming field of technology. The 21st century is going to be all about technology. The field provides the most opprotunities to the students. Our association with Top Tech giants help us to guarantee students a bright future ahead of them. Learning tech in 2023 is a key essential who is looking to scale fast and comfortably in career. Even in day to day life tech plays such a big role and keeping ourselves with technology can make our lives so much easier. So keep technologically refreshed and build the next Google with us!

Goals, Missions and Future plans

Edutech aims towards making students academically independent so that they can consume knowledge even at the comfort of their homes and maximise their efficiency. The mission of Edutech has not only to provide industry-level, carefully-oriented, modern computer subjects to our students but also to make learning affordable. We bring multiple schemes to the table for our students to avail. We arrange scolarships and full waiver on our courses for the financially challenged students. You are just away from booking a Free Class! Just check out our services and give it a go. We are eager to help you. Edutech is moving strongly to create an aducational revolution.
We have even begun to partner with world's top universities to give our students even more experienced faculty. We hope to transform Edutech into world's largest credible open school. We hope to make our students the next stalwarts in the field of technology.
